Somewhere in the Hundred acre woods lies the rearest and most hungrey of all the animals,
The Pooh Bear


Known to wear only a red shirt and often seen in company of a pig, this wild animal has been known to terroize bee hives and honey jars alike.


Other known associates are a tigger, a donkey, a rabbit, and sometimes a human child


Through his misadventures he teaches us to be friendly,thoughtful, and steadfast in our pursuits
